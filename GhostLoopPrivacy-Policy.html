Privacy Policy for Ghostloop

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Ghostloop ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and collecting only the data needed to operate and support the game.

Information We Collect Directly

Ghostloop itself does not ask you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or precise location. Your game progress — high score, in-game gems, unlocked cosmetics, daily streak, and settings — is stored locally on your device only, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Advertising

Ghostloop displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain information, including your device's advertising identifier (Advertising ID), other device identifiers, IP address, and information about your interactions with ads and the app. This information may be used to provide, personalize, and measure advertising.

The advertising in Ghostloop includes optional, opt-in "rewarded" ads that you may choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards. You are never required to watch an ad to play.

You can control ad personalization and reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device settings (on Android: Settings → Google → Ads). For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Data We Collect Through Third Parties

Through Google AdMob, the following categories of data may be collected: Device or other identifiers, and app activity and interactions with ads. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Ghostloop is intended for users aged 13 and over and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Advertising shown in the app is configured for a general (non-child) audience.

Permissions

Ghostloop does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or photos. Internet access is used to load advertisements.

Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking by adjusting your device's Advertising ID settings as described above. You can remove all locally stored game data by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date above will change when we do, and material changes will be reflected before they take effect.
